To better explain the problems with GT7's economy, estimated 'grind' and 'normal' playtimes have been added.
For 'grind', this assumes that you are extremely efficient and earn 900k Cr. per hour by doing nothing but the single current best event.
For 'normal', this assumes you're playing reasonably and earning 200k Cr. per hour but not spending a single credit until you can afford the car.

Oh, there's also some fun data with custom race payouts. These are with the player at the back of a 20 car grid on Professional difficulty.
Number of 24 hour custom races is self-explanatory. A 24 hour duration custom race pays out 122,053 Cr.
Number of Gr.3 custom race laps of the Nordschleife is calculated based on 10 lap races (which give the most efficient reward). This is 62,038 Cr. per race.
