GT7 Online Dealerships

Relating to 21-03-17 (YY-MM-DD) downtime and update changes:
While the server was down for extended maintenance after the v1.07 update,
the internet has been melting down not just because of that but also due to the credit reward changes in said update.
If you haven't seen, the most efficient way to grind is now less than half as efficient as it was before.
(A list of reward changes is available below.)

This moves the cars in the Legend Cars Dealership further out of reach for those of us with less time to play.
Of course, I disagree with this change: legendary cars were hard enough to obtain as is, and I was expecting/hoping that rewards would increase
(so that more difficult events would match the Fishermans Ranch event in value, allowing a player to rotate between many events to earn credits).
But this hasn't happened, and instead we're here.


Another update, 22-03-18 at 14:50 UTC:
All changes for today have been updated to match the GT7 server, as it is back online.
Most of the estimates were accurate. :)
 You can find a message about the downtime and reward changes from Kazunori Yamauchi, CEO of Polyphony Digital, here.
